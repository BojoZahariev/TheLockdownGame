The LOCKDOWN game

Just roll the dice and try to get to the end of the lockdown. Good luck!

You won a fight with an old lady for the last pack of toilet paper at the store. Move 3 spots forward.

You lost a fight with an old lady for the last pack of toilet paper at the store and had to buy a newspaper to use instead. Go back 3 spots.

Your Ex forgot to change the 'Netflix' password so you can still secretly use it. Move 3 spots forward.

You lost your job because of the pandemic. Go back 5 spots.

Your Ex changed the 'Netflix' password so you can't use it anymore. Go back 7 spots.

You managed to secure a free slot for groceries home delivery. Move 3 spots forward.

You couldn't find a free slot for groceries home delivery and had to go to the store. A guy sneezed on you. Go back 5 spots.

You forgot to put your trousers on before the Zoom meeting and your colleagues laughed at you. Go back 5 spots.

You finally managed to set up Zoom and participate in a meeting. Move 3 spots forward.

The gyms are closed and you don't have any fitness equipment at home so you are starting to put on weight. Go back 6 spots.

You managed to borrow a pair of rusty dumbbells from a neighbour. Time to burn all that pizza. Move 3 spots forward.

The holiday that you waited over a year for has been cancelled. Go back 6 spots.

You managed to go on holiday but now you have to self-isolate for two weeks. Go back 9 spots.

Nurseries are closed and you have to entertain your two toddlers at home all day. Go back 6 spots.

You decided to treat yourself with something from Amazon. The parcel had the virus. Go back 12 spots.

You decided to send your kids back to nursery. One of them came back with the virus. Go back 11 spots.

You went to an illegal party and contracted the virus. Go back 15 spots.

Remember that old friend you hugged the other day? He infected you. Go back 14 spots.

Remember that friend you invited for a beer because you were bored? He infected you. Go back 15 spots.

Yay! You made it to the end of the lockdown. Enjoy your new normal.
